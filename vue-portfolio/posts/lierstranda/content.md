### Sustainable Urban Design on Water

**Sustainable Urban Design on Water** is a large-scale urban project that envisions a new district built above water, integrating sustainability, digital analysis, and innovative urban planning. By prioritizing walkability, proximity to water, and mixed-use spaces, the project creates a dynamic and livable waterfront environment where residents can kayak between and through buildings in the fjord.

### Key Features

- Water-Based Urbanism: The district is lifted over water, maximizing interaction with the fjord.
- Sustainable Mobility: Designed for walkability reducing reliance on cars.
- Integrated Public Spaces Offers direct access to nature, recreation, and waterfront activities.
- Mixed-Use Development: Blends housing, commerce, and community spaces to support a vibrant urban life.
- Climate-Responsive Design: Uses digital tools to optimize environmental performance.

### Digital & Analytical Approach

- Wind & Sunlight Studies: Ensured comfortable microclimates and optimized building orientations.
- Vertical Sky Component Analysis: Maximized daylight access while maintaining urban density.
- Exploitation & Density Mapping: Balanced land use, housing capacity, and economic feasibility.
- Accessibility Analysis: Evaluated proximity to schools, food markets, and essential services to enhance livability.
- Water Circulation & Interaction: Integrated floating pathways and navigable waterways to support an active waterfront lifestyle.

### Benefits

- Resilient Urban Development: Adapts to changing environmental conditions while maximizing land use.
- Enhanced Quality of Life: Encourages social interaction, physical activity, and access to nature.
- Reduced Carbon Footprint: Walkability and sustainable transport solutions minimize emissions.
- Future-Ready Infrastructure: Digital analysis ensures long-term feasibility and optimized urban experiences.

By leveraging advanced digital tools and sustainable urban strategies, this water-based district redefines how cities interact with nature, fostering an eco-friendly, connected, and adaptable urban future.
