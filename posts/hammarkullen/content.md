### Project Description

Competition entry for a new plaza in Hammarkullen, Gothenburg. The project focuses on creating a vibrant public space that celebrates the area's diverse cultural heritage while providing new opportunities for community gatherings and activities.

### Key Features

- Integration with existing urban fabric
- Flexible spaces for markets and events
- Sustainable water management
- Local material usage
- Community-driven design process

### Process

The design process involved extensive community engagement through workshops and surveys. This helped identify key needs:

1. Better connectivity between different parts of the area
2. More green spaces
3. Improved lighting for safety
4. Flexible spaces for cultural events

### Implementation

The final design incorporates these elements through:

- Multi-level plaza design
- Interactive water features
- Integrated seating areas
- Native plantings
- Modular event spaces
